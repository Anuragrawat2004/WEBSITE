trekking

Embark on the renowned Dayara Bugyal Trek, a highlight of Uttarakhand's trekking scene.
Explore the breathtaking beauty of alpine meadows, dense forests, and majestic peaks.
Begin from Raithal village, trek through oak and rhododendron forests to Dayara Bugyal, and
witness panoramic views of Bandarpoonch and Draupadi ka Danda peaks.
Experience camping under the starlit sky at Dayara Bugyal before descending back to Raithal.
A must-do trek for nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers.
